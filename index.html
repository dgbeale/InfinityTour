Our Aim

At Infinity we aim to organise perfect romantic trips for couples looking for an exquisite getaway when celebrating an important date such as wedding anniversary, engagement, Valentine’s day or simply a different time away.

We aim to do all the very best to finding and advising clients of the most appropriate accommodation and entertainment required by their special celebration.

We liaise with the most exquisite hotels in Northern Portugal as well as Holland, Belgium and Sweden or any other region or Country of the couple’s choice.
