PORTO INFO AND ALBUM

Portugal was born in the North.

It was Porto and the Northern Region where Portugal began as nation, where the variety of complementary cultures is highly valued.

Douro River crosses the region. Enters Portugal squeezed between cliffs and Inland Mountains stepping forward across the landscape of the Worlds Heritage!

With Porto as the capital of this region offering an international airport and the charms of a riverside city splendidly bathed by the magnificent Douro River. It offers a variety of beautiful sightseeing trips on board of its traditional boats or private yachts as well as plenty of exquisite mansions or hotels for you to lodge and enjoy the aristocratic tradition of local hospitality showing a perfect alliance of Popular Heritage and Cosmopolitan elegance.
